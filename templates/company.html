About Us

South Bay Solutions (SBS) was founded in 1992. Originally named South Bay Machining, the company gained a positive reputation for manufacturing quality products while providing excellent customer service and products. In 2001, founder Adam Drewniany took the reins of this maturing company and formed a management team that has over 80 years of combined experience in high precision machining, engineering design and manufacturing assembly as well as in outsourcing service industry. South Bay Solutions now produces high quality, precision products with a customer service that is incomparable..
